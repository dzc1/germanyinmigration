About Us

We are a team of expatriates living in Germany, and who have also had to face the cumbersome bureaucracy behind every step of the immigration process. We have practically gone through it all from applying for student permits, to getting a Blue Card for working here, all the way to obtaining the German citizenship.

Our goal with EasyXpat is to simplify the German immigration process as much as posssible for other expatriates like us. Simply put, our end mission is to make the process of jumping between poorly-designed regional government websites, with information written in confusing legalese German, a thing of the past.

While we cannot support you with your specific legal case (we are not lawyers nor immigration experts, not do we offer any legal or immigration services or advice), we provide you with a set of guides and checklists to put you on the right path when living as an expat in Germany.

Whether you are just freshly arriving to Germany for your new job, or already applying for your permanent residence permit after a few years, we want to provide you with all the information you need to successfully apply for your residence permit.
